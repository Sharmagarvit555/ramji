 Our Story

Celebrating Nature's Most Precious Gifts

Since 2009, we have been the trusted destination for collectors, jewelers, and connoisseurs seeking the world's most exceptional gemstones.

 Established 2009

A Passion for Rare Beauty

RAMJI Gems was founded with a singular vision: to connect discerning collectors with nature's most extraordinary treasures. Our founder, a third-generation gemologist, spent decades traveling to the world's most remote mining regions — from the legendary Mogok Valley in Myanmar to the emerald-rich mountains of Colombia.

What began as a small consultancy advising private collectors has grown into one of the most trusted names in the fine gemstone industry. Our team of master gemologists, each with decades of experience, personally selects every stone in our inventory.

We believe every gemstone has a story — of ancient geological forces, of distant lands, of extraordinary craftsmanship. Our mission is to ensure that story continues in the hands of someone who truly appreciates its wonder.

 Our Mission

Connecting People with Earth's Wonders

Our mission is simple: to democratize access to the world's finest gemstones while maintaining the highest standards of quality, authenticity, and ethical sourcing. We believe that a truly exceptional gemstone, properly certified and ethically obtained, is not just a beautiful object — it is a piece of our planet's extraordinary history.

We are committed to education, transparency, and building long-term relationships with our clients. Whether you are a first-time buyer or a seasoned collector, we are here to guide you every step of the way.
